Industry Solutions


 
NPI Services has extensive experience across multiple industries, but we have particularly strong exposure to mining, waste processing / landfills & the energy sector.
 
Mining - Our extensive experience within the mining industry means we have a vast library of pre-configured templates for almost all types of emission-creating processes utilised on mine sites.
  • Blasting
  • Crushing
  • Drilling
  • Dumping
  • Storage tanks
  • Heavy vehicles
  • Unpaved roads
  • Wind/water erosion
  • Leach Pads
 
Waste Processing & Landfills - Our experience with a number of individual sites as well as with one of Australia's biggest waste processing corporate groups means we have an optimised set of templates including:
  • Landfill Gas Combustion
  • Natural Gas Engines
  • Flaring
  • Leachate

 
Energy - Our clients include operators of powerplants, oil and gas extraction and distribution. We have numerous common and niche processes already configured:
  • Electricity generation from Coal
  • Electricity generation from Coal Mine Gas
  • Natural Gas Engines
  • Flaring
  • Fraccing
  • Formation Water

 
We also have numerous clients across heavy industry, smelting, water treatment.
