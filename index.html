NPI Reports


 

The Australian National Pollutant Inventory reporting framework is designed to capture pollutant emissions from a large variety of industries and facilities. There are individual guides for most of these industries, but the reporting process is largely the same.

There are two primary sources of effort required to complete the NPI reports:
1. the need to retrieve and format extensive amounts of operational data for each facility
2. the expertise required to identify the correct calculation methods for each process

Both of these activities can be significantly reduced by having defined processes and access to people with the relevant experience.

NPI Services has created a solution which combines a structured process delivered through an intuitive and powerful software platform and access to an on-demand team of highly-experienced NPI consultants. Using our software platform and our extensive library of predefined process templates, you can reduce the effort required by your staff by up to 75%

The NPI Services platform is a fully transparent tool, you will be able to see exactly what calculations have been used for each process, allowing your staff to better understand the impact of each activity on your reported emissions.
